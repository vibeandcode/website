Frequently Asked Questions

What is FeelScape?
FeelScape is a personalized affirmation and mood tracker app. You select from 50+ moods across 8 emotional categories, and our AI instantly generates affirmations written specifically for how you feel right now — not generic quotes, but words tailored to your exact emotional state.
How does FeelScape personalize affirmations?
FeelScape uses AI to analyze the specific moods you select and generates affirmations that directly address your current emotional state. Premium users get an AI Memory System that learns your patterns, values, and goals over time, making affirmations even more personal and effective.
What moods can I track with FeelScape?
FeelScape offers 50+ moods organized into 8 categories: Happy, Sad, Energy, Calm, Anxious, Angry, Physical, and more. From grateful and inspired to overwhelmed and restless — there's a mood for exactly how you feel.
Is FeelScape free to use?
Yes! FeelScape is free to download and use. You get daily personalized affirmations at no cost. A premium subscription unlocks additional features like the AI Memory System, 15 daily affirmations, proactive check-ins, and unlimited mood logging.
How is FeelScape different from other affirmation apps?
Most affirmation apps give you the same recycled quotes everyone else gets. FeelScape generates affirmations written specifically for your mood, every time. Research shows personalized affirmations are 2.5x more effective than generic ones. Plus, our AI Memory System learns your emotional patterns over time for even deeper personalization.
Is my mood data private and secure?
Absolutely. Your mood data is encrypted and never sold or shared with third parties. We don't use your personal inputs to train AI models without your explicit consent. You can export or delete your data at any time. Your privacy is non-negotiable.
What is the AI Memory System?
The AI Memory System is a premium feature that learns your emotional patterns, values, goals, and coping strategies over time. It uses this understanding to generate increasingly personalized affirmations and proactive check-ins that meet you exactly where you are.
