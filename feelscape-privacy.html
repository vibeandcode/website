Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how FeelScape ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Mood selections and optional notes used to generate personalized affirmations
  • Preferences and settings within the App
  • Saved affirmations and favorites

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics and crash reports
  • General location (country/region) for service optimization

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized affirmations based on your mood selections
  • Improve and optimize the App experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features

4. AI Processing

Your inputs are processed by AI systems to generate content. This processing may occur on our servers or through third-party AI service providers. We do not use your personal inputs to train AI models without explicit consent.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. Your creations may be stored locally on your device and/or on secure cloud servers if you choose to sync across devices.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating the App
  • Legal authorities when required by law

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Export your saved data

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through our website.
