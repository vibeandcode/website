The Philosophy

"The best code is written when you're not thinking about code. It's written when you're thinking about the person who'll use it, the problem it solves, the moment of delight when everything just works."

Vibe coding isn't about being lazy or unstructured. It's about trusting the creative process, respecting your energy, and building things that genuinely matter. It's the antidote to burnout culture in tech.
